product services

Whether you have an idea, a working prototype, or paying customers, we help you get to market, faster. We thrive in the early stages of product design and development. Our product development cycle follows a lean, agile approach that goes something like this:

  • 1
  • Discover
  • We dive into your problem-space to understand what problem you're trying to solve, when and for whom.
  • 2
  • Define
  • We bring our expertise forward to start scoping your minimum viable product (MVP) solution to ensure we are clear on defining success for your product, scalability, and feasibility.
  • 3
  • Learn Quickly
  • We put pen to paper (or digital canvas) and begin building the bones of the product. We ensure every iteration undergoes user testing and we work to constantly to incorporate feedback.
  • 4
  • Build
  • With our front-end and back-end teams working concurrently, in this phase you'll see the UI become more elegant and the core of your product come to life. We'll continue our user testing cycles to iron out final details before launch.
  • 5
  • Deploy
  • Time to launch! We'll rapidly deploy code as soon as it's ready so we don't keep you or your customers, waiting. Once live, we'll monitor the health of the product by watching our KPIs (key performance indicators), closely.
  • 6
  • Rinse, Wash, Repeat
  • Building a great product is more about observation than lucky guesses. We'll be cycling through steps 3, 4 and 5 numerous times with you to land on the winning solution.

integration services

One of the most important aspects to good health products is their ability to integrate with other products. Whether your goal is to empower patients, support clinician decisions or explore big data and machine learning, we help you build bridges to the data you need to make it happen.

We have deep knowledge and experience integrating with a wide range of data types and products, such as:

Real Time Patient Feeds

Often, getting real-time patient data is a "make or break" component of your product. We leverage our connections to make integrating with an Electronic Health Record, easy. We have experience setting up an interface with all the major EHRs such as Epic and Cerner. We build integrations with scalability in mind; you shouldn't have to start from scratch for every customer.

Data Digestion (ETL) Sources

What if your product doesn't need real-time access to data, and instead needs to ingest nightly differentials from a data source? We can build a data pipeline that delivers the data you need on a predictable interval. Whether you're picking up flat files or working with a relational database, we can build a data delivery schedule that meets your needs.

Creating Secure Data Transfers

One of the most daunting tasks of setting up an integration with Protected health information (PHI) is data security. Many healthcare organizations require written and verifiable proof that your product transmits, receives, and stores protected health information with the utmost security. We can help you speed through the review process while making sure that your bases are covered.

discovery services

Do you think you have an idea for a product, but aren't sure where to start? We thrive in early stage product ideation: isolating problems and validating solutions. Our discovery process ensures you build the right thing, before putting your investment down the wrong path.

To do this, we stay lean by focusing on outcomes that give you the support needed to feel confident in front of your business stakeholders:

  • Triage
  • Whether you need help defining what to build, or you've already built a version 1, we jump in to help you put out fires and get your core structure in a more stable place to move, grow and learn.
  • MVP
  • When needed, we bring in our team of experts to assist with your specific pain points. Instead of spending your series funding on the team you think you need, we help you understand what skillsets your product and business will actually need.
  • Learn
  • The secret to building a good product, is to fail fast and learn from every chance you get in front of customers. We can help you there and show you what to look for and when to undertand your best next move as a business and for your products.
