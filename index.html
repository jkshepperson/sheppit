Spotlight market: healthcare

Healthcare is a tricky market, with lots of buyer, user and patient personas to consider on top of heavy regulation and unique workflows. Becoming an influencer in healthcare takes patience and real-world experience in hospital and clinical facilities.


Hospitals are where we've learned some of our most valuable lessons when building products, like the importance of standing shoulder-to-shoulder with your customers, and who has the real buying power in the department. Whether you need help with HIPAA, an IRB review or are just getting started - we can help you navigate the healthcare landscape.
