product services

Whether you have an idea in your head, a working protoype, or paying customers, we can help you quickly get to market. We thrive in the early stages of product design and development. Our product development cycle follows a lean and agile approach that usually goes something like this:

  • 1
  • Discover
  • We dive into your problem-space to understand what problem you're trying to solve, when and for whom.
  • 2
  • Define
  • We bring our expertise forward to start scoping your minimum viable product (MVP) solution to ensure we are clear on defining success for your product, scalability, and feasibility.
  • 3
  • Learn Quickly
  • We put pen to paper (or digital canvas) and begin building the bones of the product. We ensure every iteration undergoes user testing and we work to constantly to incorporate feedback.
  • 4
  • Build
  • With our front-end and back-end teams working concurrently, in this phase you'll see the UI become more elegant and the core of your product come to life. We'll continue our user testing cycles to iron out final details before launch.
  • 5
  • Deploy
  • Time to launch! We'll rapidly deploy code as soon as it's ready so we don't keep you or your customers, waiting. Once live, we'll monitor the health of the product by watching our KPIs (key performance indicators), closely.
  • 6
  • Rinse, Wash, Repeat
  • Building a great product is more about observation than lucky guesses. We'll be cycling through steps 3, 4 and 5 numerous times with you to land on the winning solution.

integration services

One of the most important components of your health product is how it interacts with external data. Whether your product improves the patient experience, gives clinicians real-time decision support or does big-data machine learning, we can help ensure that you get the data that you need in a secure way.

We have deep domain knowledge and experience with intergrating with all types of data and external systems.

Real Time Patient Feeds

Often, getting real-time patient data is a "make or break" component of your product. We leverage our connection with industry partners to minimize the painful experience of integrating with an electronic health record. We have experience setting up a interface with all the major EHRs. We build integrations with scalability in mind; you shouldn't have to start from scratch for every customer.

Data Digestion (ETL)

Perhaps your product doesn't need real-time access to data, and instead needs to ingest nightly differentials from a data source. We can setup a data pipeline that delivers the data you need on a predictable interval. Whether you're picking up flat files, reading from a relational database, or receving event notifications, we can ingest the data into your platform.

Security

One of the most daunting tasks of setting up an integration with Protected health information (PHI) is data security. Many healthcare organizations require written and verifiable proof that your product transmits, receives, and stores protected health information with the utmost security. We can help you speed through the review process while making sure that your bases are covered.
