ABOUT US

Hi! We are Joell Shepperson and Nick Stocchero, co-founders of Shepp It. We have dedicated our careers to building and designing practical solutions to tough problems.

Our past work includes time with Epic Systems, Aetna, Merck and Rock Health graduate, Kit Check. Our solutions focus on keeping things simple and straightforward.

When we need extra hands or specializations, we lean on our network of experts: former colleagues and specialists in their given fields who share our passion for empowering others through technology. This means, we curate each team per project to meet your exact needs, and no more.
