ABOUT US

Hi! We are Joell Shepperson and Nick Stocchero, co-founders of Shepp It. We have dedicated our careers to building and designing practical solutions in healthcare. Our resumes include past work at Epic Systems, Aetna, Merck and Rock Health graduate, Kit Check. We believe that technology should conform to the user, not the other way around. Our solutions focus on staying as simple as possible with extra care to test our projects live, in the real-world as soon as humanely possible.

When we need extra hands or specializations, we lean on our network of experts: former colleagues and specialists in their given fields who share our passion for empowering patients and provider. This means, we curate each team per project to suit your exact needs and no more.
