ABOUT US

Co-Founders Joell Shepperson and Nick Stocchero have spent over 10 years building products for startups and healthcare heavy-weights, alike. With resumes that include work at Epic Systems, Aetna, Merck and Rock Health graduate, Kit Check, Nick and Joell have dedicated their careers to building products that help people in healthcare.

When we need extra hands or specializations, we lean on our network of experts: former colleagues and specialists in their given fields who share our passion for empowering patients and provider.
